## CRISP-DM
- Cross Industry Standard for Data Mining
- A methodology for structured data driving decision making process
- Follows six stages:
  1. Business understanding
  2. Data understanding
  3. Data preparation\
  4. Modeling
  5.Evaluation
  6. Deployment
